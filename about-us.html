Our Mission

Green World For
Our Future Generation

  • Reduce Pollution: Actively reduce the amount of plastic and other pollutants entering our waterways through community clean-up events, educational programs, and partnerships with local businesses.
  • Promote Sustainable Practices: Advocate for and implement sustainable practices in industries affecting waterways, encouraging the use of environmentally friendly materials and processes.
  • Enhance Biodiversity: Work towards restoring the natural habitats in and around waterways to enhance biodiversity and support the health of aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems.
  • Educate and Engage Communities: Increase public awareness about the importance of clean waterways through outreach and educational campaigns, and engage communities in preservation efforts.
  • Support Policy and Regulation: Collaborate with policymakers to support and advocate for regulations that protect waterways from pollution and mismanagement.
  • Monitor and Research: Conduct regular monitoring and research to assess the health of waterways, and use this data to drive our cleanup initiatives and policy recommendations.
